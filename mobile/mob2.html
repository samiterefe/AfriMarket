Samsung Galaxy S4

Samsung Galaxy S4 smartphone was launched in April 2013.

  • Display: The phone comes with a 5.00-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1080 pixels by 1920 pixels at a PPI of 441 pixels per inch.
  • Performance: The Samsung Galaxy S4 is powered by 1.6GHz octa-core it comes with 2GB of RAM.
  • Version: android 4.2
  • Battery Life: powered by a 2600mAh removable battery.
  • Cost: Samsung Galaxy S4 price in India starts from Rs. 12,349.
  • Connectivity: single sim card that accepts a Micro-SIM. Connectivity options include Wi-Fi, GPS, Bluetooth, NFC, Infrared, USB OTG, 3G and 4G (with support for Band 40 used by some LTE networks in India).
  • Storage: The phone packs 16GB of internal storage that can be expanded up to 64GB via a microSD card.
