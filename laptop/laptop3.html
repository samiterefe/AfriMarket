Dell Inspiron

Dell Inspiron is a computer product line produced by Dell as a range of laptop computers targeted at entry-level consumers. Early models were equipped with Mobile Celeron or Mobile Pentium II processors and SDRAM. The Inspiron mainly competes against computers such as Acer's Aspire, Asus' Transformer Book Flip, VivoBook and Zenbook, HP's Pavilion and Envy, Lenovo's IdeaPad and Toshiba's Satellite

Details

  • Display: 15.6-inch Full HD touchscreen
  • Performance:
    • 12GB memory/1TB HDD
    • AMD A12-9700P
    • processor speed ranging between 2.1 and 2.6GHz
    • HD webcam and digital microphone.
  • Graphics: AMD Radeon R7 graphics
  • Battery Life: lasted a solid 6 hours and 37 minutes
  • Cost: starting at $500, reviewed at $700
